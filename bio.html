Josh 'Doctor' Jones

Bassist · Composer · Collaborator

About Josh

Josh "Doctor" Jones is a Wellington-based bassist and composer known for his soulful playing and collaborative spirit. With years of experience performing across New Zealand, Josh blends jazz, funk, and modern improvisation to create a distinctive voice on the upright and electric bass.

Whether in the studio or on stage, Josh brings depth, groove, and creativity to every project. He's performed with a wide range of ensembles and has contributed to numerous recordings as both a sideman and bandleader.

When he's not performing, Josh is composing original works or mentoring young musicians in the Wellington scene.
